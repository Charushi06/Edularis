Description

BITSAT is an entrance test conducted by BITS Pilani for admission to integrated first-degree programs offered at BITS Pilani, Goa, and Hyderabad campuses. The exam tests knowledge in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, English, and Logical Reasoning.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 1. 12th pass with Physics, Chemistry, Math, and adequate English proficiency
  • 2. Min 75% overall, with 60% in each subject
  • 3. Appeared for 12th in 2024 or 2025
