Description

JEE Mains is a national-level entrance exam for admission to undergraduate engineering programs in India. Conducted by NTA, it assesses candidates in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. The exam consists of two papersâ€”Paper 1 for B.Tech/ B.E and Paper 2 for B.Arch/Planning. It serves as a gateway to prestigious institutions like IITs, NITs, and other government colleges.

Exam Details

Important dates
April 2, 3, 4, 7, 8, and 9, 2025
Syllabus
NCERT curriculum for class 11 and 12. Key areas cover Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Electrostatics, Organic Chemistry, Calculus, and more.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 1. Age: Born after October 1, 2000
  • 2. 12th pass with Physics, Chemistry, and Math
  • 3. Max 3 attempts in consecutive years

Application Process

  • 1. Register: Create an account on the NTA website.
  • 2. Upload photo, signature, and ID proof.
  • 3. Pay the fee online and submit.
